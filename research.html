Antoine Renard

My research interests lie primarily in the field of combinatorics on words. My work focuses on binomial coefficients of words and related notions. On a different side, I have also explored the domain of combinatorial game theory, with a particular emphasis on the Wythoff game and some of its variants, studied using the Walnut software.

Below is a list of my publications and scientific communications (talks and posters).
