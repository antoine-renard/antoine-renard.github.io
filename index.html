Antoine Renard

Hi, I'm Antoine!

I am currently a PhD student in the Discrete Mathematics team of the Department of Mathematics at the University of Liège, Belgium. I carry out my research under the supervision of Michel Rigo and Markus A. Whiteland. Here is a short CV.

In 2022, I successfully graduated from the University of Liège with the highest distinction. I was then hired as a teaching assistant in the Department of Mathematics; an overview of my teaching activities over the past few years can be found here. Meanwhile, I began my PhD in the field of discrete mathematics and combinatorics on words. My research focuses on binomial coefficients of words and related topics. More recently, I have also explored the use of the Walnut software in the context of combinatorial game theory. A list of my publications and other scientific communications is available here.
